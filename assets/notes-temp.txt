When you click on a particular county, the following information will be available (in numbers or percentages):
# of juvenile lifers resentenced
# of juvenile lifers released
# of juvenile lifers resentenced to non-life terms
# of juvenile lifers resentenced to life without parole
# cases still pending resentencing

For example,
Philadelphia had 304 total juvenile lifers.
275 total resentanced
55.9% or 170 juvenile lifers from Philadelphia have been released since 2016.
32.9% or 100 juvenile lifers have been re-sentenced to non-life terms.
1.3% or 4 juvenile lifers have been given life without parole again.
8.9% or 27 juvenile lifers are still waiting for their resentencing.
