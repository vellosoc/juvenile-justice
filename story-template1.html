Juvenile Justice in Philadelphia


Philadelphia had the largest juvenile lifer population of any jurisdiction across the United States at 304.

288 of the juvenile lifers were black or hispanic.

16 were white.

Among the juvenile population, only 4 women had been sentenced to life without parole.

Since 2016, when the Supreme Court ruled mandatory life sentences for juveniles unconsitutional, Philadelphia has released 170 juvenile lifers.

27 juvenile lifers are pending resentencing.

104 have been resentenced.

100 have been resentenced to non-life terms and 4 have been resentenced to life.
